Achoran Parties Merge as Election Tensions Rise!

Saturday, March 1st, 2025

This Saturday morning the Freedom Party and the National Party came together and merged under the leadership of Trajan. They did this in order to attempt to gain a competitive advantage against the Red Dawn and Gruntists. The Freedom and National parties had previously held roughly similar positions on “Ostojanomics” and the role of the free market, so they agreed to set aside their other differences (largely on the size of government, with the Freedom party favoring a small government, and the National party one much larger) to defeat the economic interventionists.

However, this advantage did not last: just a few hours later, the Red Dawn and Gruntists came together with an agreement of their own, merging under Amboush. Thus, this election has become a two-party election between the two parties, largely on economic grounds. The nation will choose tomorrow whether it wishes to have a free market upon established Economic principle with Trajan, or pursue an entirely new direction under Amboush.
