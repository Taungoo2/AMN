Open Letter on the Voting System

Sunday, March 2nd, 2025

In light of the current election the following letter has been prepared and signed:

To whom it may concern,

Furthermore it takes away the right to privacy and confidentiality held by man in his natural state. For this reason while we may vote, this letter of condemnation on this vote has been written to show public discontent with it.
